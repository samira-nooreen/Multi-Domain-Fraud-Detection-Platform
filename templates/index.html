Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our fraud detection platform

What types of fraud can your platform detect?

Our platform detects various types of fraud including UPI fraud, credit card fraud, loan defaults, insurance fraud, click fraud, fake news, spam emails, phishing URLs, fake profiles/bots, and document forgery. We use advanced machine learning algorithms tailored to each domain.

How accurate are your fraud detection models?

Our models achieve industry-leading accuracy rates, typically above 95% for most fraud types. We continuously retrain our models with new data to maintain and improve performance. Accuracy varies by fraud type and data quality.

How quickly can I get results from your system?

Most fraud detection analyses are completed in real-time, typically within milliseconds. For complex analyses like document forgery, results are usually available within seconds. Our system is designed for high throughput with low latency.

Is my data secure when using your platform?

Absolutely. We implement bank-level security measures including end-to-end encryption, secure data storage, and strict access controls. All data is processed in secure environments and never shared with third parties without explicit consent.

Can I integrate your solution with my existing systems?

Yes, our platform offers robust APIs and SDKs for easy integration with existing systems. We support various integration methods including REST APIs, webhooks, and direct database connections. Our team provides full support during the integration process.

How often do you update your fraud detection models?

We update our models regularly based on new fraud patterns and emerging threats. Critical updates are deployed immediately, while major model retraining occurs monthly. We also provide real-time adaptive learning capabilities for some modules.
